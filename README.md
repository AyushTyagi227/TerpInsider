# TerpInsider
Repository for the TerpInsider WebPage

Problem:
Student involvement in on-campus clubs and organizations is a crucial part of college life. In a large college such as the University of Maryland, there are hundreds of clubs and organizations students can get involved in. However, there is currently no effective way to explore and discover new organizations from an unbiased, third-party perspective. Current university websites contain outdated, biased information and are inefficient in guiding students towards their passions and interests.

Solution:
TerpInsider is an online platform that will serve as a tool for students at the University of Maryland, allowing them to discover new clubs and organizations that align with their interests. A primary feature of the platform is the student review forum. Students who have participated in a particular organization can leave ratings, reviews, and feedback, guiding other prospective members. This platform will effectively solve this current lack of functionality and make the University of Maryland a more connected and inclusive campus.
